Featured Project

Capstone: HW Dental

MS Business / Data Analytics · Capstone Project · 2025

Patient Acquisition Analytics Across Multi-Branch Dental Practice

4,546 new patients · 4 branches · Demographic, insurance, referral & geographic data

The Data Journey

01

Problem

HW Dental was experiencing rapid growth across multiple branches but lacked clear visibility into which marketing channels, patient segments, and geographic areas were most effectively driving new patient acquisition.

02

Data

A dataset of 4,546 new patients across four branches, including demographic, insurance, referral source, and geographic information — providing a comprehensive view of acquisition patterns at scale.

03

Journey

Data was cleaned, standardized, and enriched through feature engineering — including age grouping, Medicaid indicators, and referral categorization — then subjected to exploratory and comparative analysis across branches, channels, and segments.

04

Solution

Targeted recommendations were developed to optimize marketing channel allocation, improve outreach to key populations, and concentrate resources on high-potential geographic areas identified in the analysis.

Problem Statement

Despite rapid multi-branch expansion, HW Dental had no unified framework to evaluate which marketing investments were generating patient growth — creating the risk of budget inefficiency and missed acquisition opportunities in key demographic segments.

Analytical Approach

Applied ETL workflows to clean and standardize raw intake data, then engineered features such as age bands, Medicaid eligibility flags, and referral channel groupings to enable meaningful cross-branch comparisons and segment-level analysis.

Key Findings

Identified which referral channels delivered the highest-value patient segments, uncovered geographic clusters with untapped acquisition potential, and revealed insurance-based disparities in patient distribution across branch locations.

Impact & Recommendations

Delivered actionable channel allocation strategies, population-specific outreach recommendations, and a geographic focus map — enabling HW Dental to pursue sustainable, data-driven growth across all four locations.
